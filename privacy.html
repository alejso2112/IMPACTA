Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how IMPACTA Business Solutions Inc. ("IMPACTA," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website at https://impactabusiness.solutions and use our Services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

A. Information You Provide Directly

This includes Personal Data you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Book a diagnostic or service (e.g., the $350 Revenue Leak Diagnostic).

  • Contact us via email or phone.

  • Fill out forms on the Website.

Types of Data Collected:

  • Contact Data: Name, email address (support@impactabusiness.solutions), and phone number ((352) 389-1832).

  • Transactional Data: Payment details (processed by a third party, see Section 3), and service purchase history.

B. Information Collected Automatically (Usage Data)

When you visit the Website, we automatically collect data about your device and activity.

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and mobile network information.

  • Tracking Data: Information collected via cookies, pixel tags, and similar technologies (e.g., time spent on pages, referral sources).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following primary purposes:

  • To Provide Services: To audit your sales funnel, find bottlenecks, and deliver the custom action plan and PPA model as part of the Revenue Leak Diagnostic.

  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries, send service confirmations, and communicate about your account.

  • Processing Transactions: To facilitate and process payments for services booked via the Website (e.g., through our Shopify integration). We do not store full payment card details.

  • Marketing & Improvement: To analyze website usage, improve the Website and Services, and deliver targeted marketing communications about IMPACTA services.

4. Sharing Your Information (Third-Party Disclosure)

We may share your information with the following categories of third parties:

  • Service Providers: We use third-party tools and platforms to operate our business, such as web hosting, analytics providers, and the Shopify platform for e-commerce functionality (managing the product component). These providers are restricted from using your Personal Data for any purpose other than providing services to IMPACTA.

  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect our rights or property, or ensure the safety of our users.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures designed to protect your Personal Data from accidental loss and unauthorized access, use, alteration, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your Personal Data:

  • Access: The right to request copies of your Personal Data.

  • Rectification: The right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or incomplete.

  • Erasure: The right to request that we erase your Personal Data, under certain conditions.

  • Opt-out of Marketing: The right to opt out of receiving marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 7.
